About

STOMP Urban Dance Competition and Showcase is a spectacular event celebrating it's aniversary.

Dancers and performers ranging from 6-24 years of age will demonstrate their interpretation of various dances such as hip hop, bollywood, contemporary, jazz, dance hall, ballet, and step.

This one day event provides an opportunity for participants to perform on a world reowned stage of Meridian Hall Peforming Arts with family and friends cheering them on with a pride.
